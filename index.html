Privacy Policy for Heart of Deeds

Last Updated: October 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Heart of Deeds (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Heart of Deeds mobile application (the “App”).

Information We Collect

Heart of Deeds does not collect or share personal data such as your name, email, or address. The app only stores progress locally on your device — including your deeds, streaks, and balance data — to help you track your journey.

How We Use Data

The app uses local data storage (SharedPreferences) solely to save your own progress. No information is uploaded or transmitted to external servers.

Third-Party Services

Heart of Deeds does not use analytics, advertisements, or any third-party tracking systems.

Your Data Control

You can reset all app data anytime from the reset option inside the app.

This policy may be updated occasionally to reflect improvements. You will always find the latest version here.
